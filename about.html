About ZLN Seminars

ZLN Seminars, LLC is a workshop, seminar and conference hosting and facilitating resource. ZLN Seminars also hosts its own seminar series entitled Soul Work.The
Soul Work Series, is an interactive life-coaching sequence of workshops, designed to assist individuals in an understanding of spiritual identity and life passion/purpose, as these two areas relate to the attainment of a fulfilling vocation and occupation. ZLN Seminars also provides hosting and facilitation services to non-profit organizations, Faith-Based organizations and Start-up businesses.
