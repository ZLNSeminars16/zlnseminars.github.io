Naked and Ashamed

by Moya E. Brathwaite

Posted on October 13, 2017 at 08:58 PM

Fear of being unmasked, unveiled and emotionally vulnerable hinders any movement toward unification with “True Self.” Belief in separation is ego’s way of maintaining a false desire to be disconnected. Interconnections are fundamental to developing and sustaining relationships, including a honest relationship with self. Release fear, change beliefs, refine life experiences.
-Moya B. Soul Work

Soul Light

by Moya E. Brathwaite

Posted on September 25, 2017 at 09:02 PM

There is a spark of light in every soul. To become attuned to the essence of “True Self,” it is necessary to connect with the spark, allowing it to flare, expand and strip away extraneous mental and emotional veils. Let your soul light energize, rejuvenate and renew YOU.
-Moya B. Soul Work

Seek

by Moya E. Brathwaite

Posted on August 28, 2017 at 08:01 PM

Soul Work is an inner journey. It requires a willingness to dedicate a block of time for the purpose of being still, being meditative and being reflective. We may not be able to completely withdraw, but we have to secure the time and create the space necessary to turn inward and listen to the sounds of our souls. Seeking “true self” begins the process of individuation. We begin to discover who we are, our reason for being and our position in the plan.
-Moya B. Soul Work
