About ZLN Seminars

ZLN Seminars is a workshop and seminar resource, providing
hosting and facilitation services to Non-profit organizations,
Faith-Based organizations and Start-up businesses. ZLN Seminars
also hosts its own seminar series entitled Soul Work.The Soul Work
Series, is an interactive life-coaching series of workshops designed
to assist individuals in an understanding of spiritual identity and life
passion/purpose, as these two areas relate to the attainment of a
fulfilling vocation and occupation.
